Tribute to A.P.J.ABDUL KALAM

About A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

A.P.J Abdul Kalam ,the "Missile Man of India" was born on october 15,1931.in Rameswaram,tamilnadu.He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, know for his Insia's space and missile programs.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicles(SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear teste in 1998.
  • Wrote books "Wings of fire and India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.
